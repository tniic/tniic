TNIIC

Identifying individuals with a penchant for creating solutions to existing problems is the need of the hour, and this mind-set must be identified at a very early age. That is precisely what the Tamil Nadu Ilam Innovators Challenge (TNIIC) is designed to do. By partnering with active Start-Ups, we seek young minds bent towards entrepreneurship and innovation, and show them the path to forge their own futures.

TANSIM & EDII

The Tamil Nadu Start-Up and Innovation Mission with the aid of its nodal agency, the Entrepreneurship Development and Innovation Institute, aims to create and sustain an ecosystem that is beneficial to entrepreneurs.
‍
Through the TNIIC, we aim to foster creativity and nurture a problem solving mind-set through gamified activities, which go against the grain of conventional pedagogical methods.

Train The Trainers (TTT)

  • Rather than making students sit in a room and make people lecture on the benefits of innovation and entrepreneurship, we take a different approach, by giving the students from colleges the chance to train and mentor school students.
  • They will be selected through a screening process and appointed as District Captains, who will be oriented towards motivating the students to be more creative and innovative.
  • The first TTT Programme was conducted in Chennai on 28th May, 2019. Following the first TTT programme, the next three programmes will be held in Trichy, Madurai and Coimbatore during second and third week of June, 2019
  • This programme will take place over the course of a day with fifty participants for each session. A total of 200 E-Cell members will be trained to become District Captains.
  • The District Captains (2 Nos.) will facilitate Stage 1 of TNIIC-2019 at each Educational District.

Three Stages of TNIIC

Gamified Selection

  • Since the usual methods will not aid in enhancing or assessing the innovative skills of the students, TANSIM and EDII-TN in collaboration with the partner Start-Ups, have created a series of gamified activities, to engage the students.
  • The Principal of each school in the Educational District will nominate a team of 3 Students (8th to 12th Standard) and will complete the registration online.
  • 1 Team per School → 10 Schools i.e 10 Teams in a Education District, will assemble at the venue and the District Captain will start the Gamified Selection Process.
  • The participants will be evaluated based on their communication and collaboration with other members of their team, and also their ability to think creatively and critically.
  • 3 out of 10 Teams from 52 Educational Districts in the State of Tamil Nadu, will move on to Stage 2.

Workshop

  • During the four day residential workshop, with all the partner start-ups, TANSIM and EDII-TN will organise more activities each day, which will focus on honing the attributes that comprise the mind of an ideal entrepreneur, such as Empathy, identifying and solving problems along with presentation skills.
  • The teams that have made it this far will be mentored by their captains and the start-ups in identifying problems around them and designing solutions for the same. They will pitch these solutions on the fourth day of the workshop, where they will be judged by Jury, the partner Start-Ups, TANSIM and EDII-TN. Finally three teams from each zone will be selected for a Final Pitch at Chennai.
  • The teams that have been selected as winners will be awarded a District Level Award of Rs. 10,000/- each. They will also be provided with a month’s time for developing a viable prototype and solution, which they will pitch in the final stage.

Pitch-fest

  • 15 teams will be selected for the final round of the challenge. Their progress in developing a prototype/solution will be monitored by the Captains with weekly reviews. The students may make use of the ATAL Tinkering Labs and SP Robotic Works Makerspaces to work on their prototype.
  • They will present their prototype/solution to a jury and they will select five teams will be selected as the winners of the TAMIL NADU ILAM INNOVATORS CHALLENGE 2019.
  • The winners will be awarded a State Level Fellowship of Rs. 1,00,000/- each and the Captains who aided them will get credit vouchers worth Rs. 10,000/-
