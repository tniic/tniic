    TAMIL NADU ILAM INNOVATORS CHALLENGE

    Tamil Nadu Ilam Innovators Challenge is an initiative of the Tamil Nadu Startup and Innovation Mission (TANSIM), Entrepreneurship Development and Innovation Institute (EDII-TN), along with the partner Startups - MADIEE, Infinite Engineers, WarHorse, SP Robotic Works, E21 and Getset Ventures, along with the support of the Directorate of School Education, GoTN.

    Know More

    OBJECTIVE

    · To nurture imagination, creativity and innovation amongst the school students of TN.
    · To inculcate entrepreneurial skills in the hearts of the students, such as Empathy, innovation, creativity and problem solving, to make them future ready.

    Know More

    PURPOSE

    The TNIIC aims to bridge the skill gap that exists among young minds. By prioritizing innovation we pique the curiosity of students, and make them identify problems and solve them. Through the TNIIC, students across the state will explore the concept of innovation and entrepreneurship.

    About Us

    Established in 2001, the Entrepreneurship Development and Innovation Institute of Tamil Nadu (EDII-TN) exists to empower entrepreneurs and build an ecosystem that sustains and nourishes entrepreneurship. EDII-TN also acts as the nodal agency for the Tamil Nadu Start-Up and Innovation Mission (TANSIM).
    EDII-TN and TANSIM have partnered with various Start-Ups, and created the Tamil Nadu Ilam Innovators Challenge (TNIIC), which is aimed at developing innovation and entrepreneurial skills among school students.

    Register

    The Principal/Headmaster of the School is requested to nominate 1 team of 3 students (8th to 12th Standard), who have already participated in science fairs and other competitions and have shown creativity and interest in innovation during classes. In each Education District, 10 teams (1 team per school) will participate in Stage 1 out of which 3 teams will be selected for Stage 2.

    FAQs

    Who can participate?
    TNIIC is open to all school students(8th - 12th Standards) with a flair for innovation and entrepreneurship. We aim to have more than 1500 students from 500+ schools, both public and private, in Tamil Nadu.
    Why should you participate?
    The TNIIC provides an opportunity for students with a creative edge to showcase their innovative prowess. Though only five teams will get selected in the end, more than 450 students will be shown the road to innovation and entrepreneurship. Through their exposure to the activities in the TNIIC and facilities like Makerspaces and the ATAL Tinkering labs, some might even opt for careers as entrepreneurs.
    Do I win anything?
    • At STAGE 2, 15 teams will be awarded a district level award of Rs 10,000/- each.
    • The five winning teams of STAGE 3 will be awarded a state level fellowship of Rs. 1,00,000 each.
    • The District captains who mentor the winning teams will be awarded credit vouchers worth Rs. 10,000/- each.
    How many members can be in a team?
    The TNIIC allows a maximum of three members per team.
    What should I bring?
    The Students don’t have to bring anything, except lunch for STAGE 1. All materials will be provided for them on the spot by their captains and the partner Start-Ups.
    Will I be reimbursed for my travelling?
    EDII-TN does not provide any reimbursement for the team’s travel bills. However EDII-TN will provide food and board for the four day workshop and the final Pitchfest.
    When will registrations close?
    The last date to register has been extended to August 14th, 2019, to cover all the Districts of Tamil Nadu State. Once registration has been completed, you will receive an email confirming your registration.
    Where does it take place?
    The TNIIC is spread out over five zones in the state, namely Chennai, Coimbatore, Madurai, Trichy and Tirunelveli. A zone will include all districts in proximity to it. For instance the Chennai zone will include, Chennai, Villupuram, Thiruvannamalai, Kanchipuram, Vellore, Tiruvallur and Chennai.
    How Can I Register:
    The registration for the TNIIC can be done through this form.
    Can I apply individually?
    All the 3 stages of the TNIIC are team events. Applying as an individual is not possible.
    How much does it cost?
    There is no entry or registration fees for any stages of the TNIIC.
